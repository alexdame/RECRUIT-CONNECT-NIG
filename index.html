Meet Our Leaders

Tiamiyu Oluwaseun Michael

Founder & Principal Talent Consultant

Tiamiyu Oluwaseun Michael is a Human Resources and Talent Acquisition expert and the Founder of Recruitconnect Nig Ltd, a firm dedicated to helping organizations attract, hire, and retain high-performing professionals. He specializes in building structured recruitment frameworks that allow organizations to scale efficiently with the right talent.

With extensive experience across the entire talent acquisition lifecycle—covering workforce planning, candidate sourcing, competency-based interviewing, onboarding, and performance management—Oluwaseun partners with leadership teams to align talent strategy with organizational goals.

Through Recruitconnect Nig Ltd, he supports startups, SMEs, and growing companies, providing recruitment solutions that enhance hiring efficiency while maintaining high candidate quality. He is also committed to talent development, offering mentorship and professional guidance to job seekers and professionals.

Blessing Nnenna Okorie

Co-Founder & Human Resources Consultant

Blessing Okorie is a Human Resources professional and Co-Founder of Recruitconnect Nig Ltd. She is passionate about designing structured HR systems that support organizational growth and enhance employee experience.

Blessing holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics (Second Class Upper Division) from Ambrose Alli University and is pursuing an MBA in Human Resources at Ahmadu Bello University. She also holds the globally recognized Professional in Human Resources – International (PHRi) certification.

Before co-founding Recruitconnect Nig Ltd, Blessing gained extensive experience in administrative leadership and HR operations, specializing in recruitment coordination, onboarding, performance management, and employee engagement. At Recruitconnect, she develops HR support systems and recruitment processes that help organizations hire, retain, and develop top talent.

Blessing is dedicated to building productive workplaces where individuals and organizations can thrive through effective HR practices and structured talent management.
